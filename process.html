// THE PROCESS

How Working Together
Actually Works.

From first message to final delivery. No vague agreements. No disappearing acts. No missed deadlines without advance communication. Clear terms before anything starts.

// STEP BY STEP
INITIAL CONTACT

Send a message through the contact page or directly to forge@fluidfortune.com. Describe the project, the timeline, and the budget range if you have one. No commitment required. No form required. An email is enough.

DISCOVERY CALL (30 MINUTES)

A focused conversation to understand the specific situation, what is needed, and whether this is a good fit. If it is not a good fit, you will hear that honestly — along with a referral if possible. No sales pressure. A conversation.

SCOPE PROPOSAL

A written document defining the work, the deliverable, the timeline, and the price. No vague agreements. Clear terms before anything starts. Both parties sign off before work begins.

EXECUTION

Work begins. Updates at agreed checkpoints. The forge does not disappear between milestones. If something unexpected comes up that affects the timeline or scope, you hear about it before it becomes a problem.

DELIVERY & REVIEW

The deliverable is delivered. One round of revisions is included in all standard engagements. Additional revision rounds are available at the hourly rate. Revisions are for refinement — not for scope expansion.

COMPLETION & FOLLOW-UP

Final delivery, invoice, and a brief follow-up to confirm the work landed correctly. If the project leads naturally to future work, that conversation happens here. No pressure either way.

// WORKING STYLE
REMOTE. GLOBAL. ASYNC-FIRST.

The forge operates remotely and works with clients globally. Communication defaults to async — email and written updates — with calls scheduled when voice adds genuine value. Time zones are not an obstacle.

DEFINED SCOPE. NO DRIFT.

Every engagement has a written scope document. Scope changes are discussed and priced before implementation. This protects your budget and the forge's time.

HONEST TIMELINES.

If a timeline is not achievable, the discovery call is where that gets said. Rush work is available at a premium rate. Impossible deadlines are declined rather than missed.

RESPONSE WITHIN 48 HOURS.

All inquiries receive a response within 48 hours. Typically faster. If your project has a tight deadline, note it in the initial message.

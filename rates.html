On pricing: These are starting points, not rigid menus. Complex projects get custom quotes. Simple projects sometimes fit a flat rate. If your project is outside these ranges, say so in the initial message.

// PAYMENT TERMS

Standard terms: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery for project-based work. Monthly retainers invoiced at the start of each month. One-time consultations invoiced after the call with net-7 payment terms. Preferred payment: wire transfer or PayPal. Invoices issued in USD. Rates in other currencies quoted on request.
